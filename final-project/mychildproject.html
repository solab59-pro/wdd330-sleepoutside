The 11 MyChild Believe

Every child is a beloved son or daughter of God, born to fulfill a purpose on earth and destined for greatness

Every child needs to discover, develop and deploy their gifts and talents in order to fulfill their purpose on earth

Each child is unique and has the privilege of receiving personal revelation from God for his or her own life.

Every child deserves the privilege and opportunity to be raised by both parents in a home of love, peace and unity

Parents are the primary role model of their children, hence, ought to live worthy of emulation

Parents need to regularly counsel with their children and share positive advice with them as they go through different phases of life. They need to know and feel that they are not alone in this life

A healthy balanced life is achieved as each child grow spiritually, intellectually, socially and physically

Education is vital and helps to create a better future for the children

Service to humanity is essential and help each child become a nation builder

Every child needs to be taught to be law abiding citizen of their country and wherever they live

This season bring with it some of the greatest challenges and opportunities and every child is born for the season
